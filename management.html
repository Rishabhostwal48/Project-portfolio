Our Founder

"Since founding BondSure Infra in 1992, our north star has remained unchanged — to build infrastructure that endures, that serves communities, and that reflects the highest standards of engineering integrity. Every bridge we raise, every road we lay, and every dam we construct carries our promise to India's future."

With over 35 years of experience in civil engineering and infrastructure development, our Chairman has guided the company from a regional contractor to one of Central India's most respected construction firms.
