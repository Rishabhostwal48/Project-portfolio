Irrigation Projects

Transforming water availability across drought-prone regions through canals, reservoirs, and lift irrigation systems since 2002.

Chambal Canal Phase III

68km main canal with 22 distributaries serving 18,000+ hectares in Morena district. Value: ₹142 Cr.

Earth-fill storage dam with 8.4 MCM capacity including spillway and sluice works. Value: ₹78 Cr.

Multi-stage pumping system serving 8,200 hectares in Rajgarh. Value: ₹88 Cr.

Betwa River Check Dams — Series of 12

12 check dams along the Betwa River for groundwater recharge and minor irrigation. Total value: ₹36 Cr.
