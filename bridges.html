Bridges & Structures

Engineering feats that connect communities — major bridges, flyovers, and structural works across Central India.

Major Bridge · 2022

Narmada River Cable-Stayed Bridge

320m cable-stayed bridge on SH-22 near Hoshangabad — the longest in our portfolio. Value: ₹112 Cr.

Flyover · 2021

Indore Airport Road Flyover

1.2km elevated flyover with 3 spans over railway crossing on the Indore airport approach road. Value: ₹84 Cr.

ROB · 2020

Road Over Bridge — Bhopal North

4-lane ROB eliminating a level crossing on BG railway track serving 18,000 vehicles/day. Value: ₹48 Cr.

Minor Bridges · 2022

NH-47 Package — 14 Minor Bridges

14 minor bridges with spans of 6m–60m as part of the NH-47 four-laning project. Aggregate value: ₹32 Cr.
