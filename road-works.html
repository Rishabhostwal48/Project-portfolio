Road Works

From national highways to rural roads, BondSure Infra has built 2,800+ km of road infrastructure across Central India.

National Highway · 2023

NH-47 Four-Lane Expansion

42km four-laning with 8 grade separators and 14 minor bridges through Madhya Pradesh. Value: ₹186 Cr.

State Highway · 2020

SH-18 Upgradation — 96km

Widening and resurfacing of SH-18 with full drainage restoration and safety infrastructure. Value: ₹64 Cr.

PMGSY · 2021

Rural Road Connectivity — Barwani Cluster

Construction of 140km all-weather rural roads connecting 48 villages under PM Gram Sadak Yojana. Value: ₹28 Cr.

Urban Roads · 2022

Indore Ring Road Phase II

6-lane urban arterial road with utility ducting, cycle tracks, and landscaped medians. Length: 18km. Value: ₹72 Cr.

Bypass · 2019

Ujjain City Bypass — 24km

Four-lane bypass to divert national highway traffic around Ujjain city, improving safety and reducing congestion. Value: ₹95 Cr.

Expressway · 2023

MP Expressway Package — 38km

Design-build package for 38km access-controlled expressway with toll plazas, emergency laybys, and ITS systems. Value: ₹210 Cr.
