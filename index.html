About Vertex

Engineering India's Growth, One Project at a Time

Vertex Construction & Infrastructure Pvt. Ltd. is a premier engineering and construction firm committed to driving India's ambitious infrastructure vision. With deep expertise across roads, irrigation systems, dams, power facilities, and affordable housing, we play a pivotal role in shaping the nation's future.

Based in the heart of Central India, Vertex has rapidly gained recognition for its commitment to excellence, safety, and innovation — delivering high-quality projects on time and within budget.
