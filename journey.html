Our Journey

From humble beginnings in 1992 to becoming one of Central India's most respected infrastructure builders — a story of grit, vision, and relentless growth.

The BondSure Story

Three Decades,
One Mission

What began as a small civil contracting firm has grown into a full-spectrum infrastructure powerhouse. Our journey is defined by persistence, quality, and a deep belief that great infrastructure transforms lives.

1992 — Foundation Year

BondSure Infra is Born

Founded in Indore with a small team of 12 engineers and a vision to build reliable infrastructure for growing India. First project: a rural road under PMGSY in Madhya Pradesh.

1997 — Expansion

First Major Government Contract

Secured first state PWD contract for district road upgradation across 3 districts in MP. Expanded team to 45 professionals and acquired core equipment fleet.

2002 — Diversification

Entering Irrigation Sector

Successfully completed first irrigation canal project under Narmada Valley Development Authority, opening a new chapter in water infrastructure.

2008 — Milestones

100+ Projects Completed

Crossed the landmark of 100 completed projects. Received ISO 9001 certification, formalising quality management processes across all operations.

2013 — National Scale

First National Highway Project

Empanelled as NHAI vendor and awarded first national highway package — a 42km stretch of NH-12, marking entry into national-scale road infrastructure.

2018 — Innovation

Technology-Led Construction

Launched in-house BIM lab and drone survey unit. Became one of the first regional contractors to adopt Building Information Modelling for project planning.

2022 — Recognition

National Award Winner

Received the CIDC Best Infrastructure Contractor award. Workforce expanded to 120+ professionals. Active project portfolio crossed ₹800 Crore.

2026 — Today

Leading Central India's Growth

180+ completed projects, 60+ client partners, operations across 6 states. BondSure Infra continues to build the infrastructure that India's future depends upon.
