Equipment Details

Our Key Equipment Includes

⚙️ Excavators 210

Heavy-duty excavators used for digging, trenching, and large-scale earthmoving tasks. Equipped with various attachments for different soil types and excavation depths. Ideal for foundation work, drainage trenching, and site clearing.

🚛 10-Wheel Tippers

High-capacity tipper trucks essential for transporting soil, debris, sand, aggregates, and construction materials quickly and effectively between sites. Ensures smooth material flow and reduces project downtime.

🔧 Tractor Compressors

Used for compacting soil layers during earthwork and road base preparation. Ensures the required density and stability before laying concrete or other surface materials, critical for RCC road quality.

🟡 Backhoe Loader (JCB)

Versatile construction machine used for digging, trenching, material shifting, and backfilling. The JCB is a core asset on every site — equally capable for utility trenching, foundation work, and material handling.
